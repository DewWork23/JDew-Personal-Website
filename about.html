Jordan combines his passion for technology and social impact.

As a Social Research Specialist at UNC Pembroke, Jordan works on developing strategic plans for distributing Robeson County's Opioid Settlement Funding. His work involves community engagement, data collection, and collaboration with local government and health organizations to address substance use disorders in the region.

With a background as an IT Specialist at Southern Software Inc., he brings technical expertise in software management and data systems to his social research work. His projects include developing a community resource guide at Robeson.help and creating a CRM system for Changing Destinies Ministry.

Currently pursuing a Master of Social Work at UNC Pembroke, Jordan combines his IT background with social work training to apply data-driven approaches to community health challenges. He also holds a certification in Big Data For Social Good from Harvard Online.

When he's not working or studying, you'll find Jordan enjoying a game of chess, throwing discs at the local disc golf course, or playing Call of Duty with his wife.

Passionate about making a difference, Jordan welcomes connections with others who share his interest in technology, social work, or both. Feel free to reach out!
