Opioid Settlement Strategic Planning

Research & Policy

Project Overview

Contributing to the strategic planning process for distributing Robeson County's Opioid Settlement Funding. This work involves community engagement, data collection, and policy development to ensure effective use of settlement funds for addressing substance use disorders.

Key Contributions

  • Consolidated Impact Report for State Submission
  • Community Focus Groups & Surveys
  • Data Analysis & Strategic Recommendations
  • Stakeholder Coordination with RCORP Consortium
